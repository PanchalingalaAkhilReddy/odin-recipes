Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and creamy bechamel. It's rich, hearty, and perfect for family dinners.

This version uses ground beef, mozzarella, and a homemade tomato sauce for a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Sauté garlic and onion in olive oil, then add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  6. Repeat layers and top with parmesan cheese.
  7. Bake for 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
